**Objectives of the Study**
The main objective of the study is to design and develop BlockFlow, a management and analytics system for Magalin Hollow Blocks Trading. 
1. Implement an inventory management that:
1.1 Record overall expenses of raw material, maintenance, and other expenses.
1.2 Record current available stock
1.3 Record daily sales
2. Develop a specialized analytical tool that:
2.1. Utilize statistical measures of central tendency and dispersion to identify peak monthly performance.
2.2. Implement an autoregressive model to forecast future sales by analyzing historical transaction patterns.
2.3. Integrate an AI reporting feature to generate daily, monthly, or quarterly sales and expense reports with prescriptive business recommendations. 
3. Design and develop a centralized dashboard for the BlockFlow system that displays:
3.1 Current stock levels
3.2 Daily sales and overall expenses
3.3 Profit calculation feature

**Use Case Diagram**
   This figure presents the use case diagram of BlockFlow. The diagram illustrates the interaction between the owner, staff and the major functions of the proposed system. The staff can access the system through the login feature and view the dashboard, which provides an overview of inventory, sales, expenses, and other business-related information, because the staff only are responsible for handling and monitoring all information and transactions of the business the owner has only been there once so he does not know how the business operates. Even though the decision of all needed for the success of the business is on the owner. 
   Also, the system enables the owner to login, view dashboard and it also allows management of all expenses related to production planning and daily operations. The owner can access to generate sales forecasts to help improve decision-making and production planning. The system can provide insights that help the business evaluate sales performance, inventory usage, and operational efficiency and generate reports.
   Additionally, the use case diagram shows how BlockFlow integrates inventory, production, and sales management processes into one system to help Magalin Hollow Blocks Trading improve efficiency, accuracy, and overall business operations.
