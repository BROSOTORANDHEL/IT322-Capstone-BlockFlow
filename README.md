**Objectives of the Study**
The main objective of the study is to design and develop BlockFlow, a management and analytics system for Magalin Hollow Blocks Trading. 
1. Implement an inventory management that:
1.1 Record overall expenses of raw material, maintenance, and other expenses.
1.2 Record current available stock
1.3 Record daily sales
2. Develop a specialized analytical tool that:
2.1. Utilize statistical measures of central tendency and dispersion to identify peak monthly performance.
2.2. Implement an autoregressive model to forecast future sales by analyzing historical transaction patterns.
2.3. Integrate an AI reporting feature to generate daily, monthly, or quarterly sales and expense reports with prescriptive business recommendations. 
3. Design and develop a centralized dashboard for the BlockFlow system that displays:
3.1 Current stock levels
3.2 Daily sales and overall expenses
3.3 Profit calculation feature

   Also, the system enables the owner to login, view dashboard and it also allows management of all expenses related to production planning and daily operations. The system can provide insights that help the business evaluate sales performance, inventory usage, and operational efficiency and generate reports.
